ECMAScript Specification

Defines the standard (Rules) for the implementation of JavaScript.

How JavaScript evolves

JavaScript was invented by Brendan Eich in 1995 (in just 10 days). It was developed for netscape browser.

It was originally named Mocha, but quickly became known as liveScript and later JavaScript.

With popularity gain Netscape handed Javascript over to ECMA. Now ECMA foundation took the responsibility to standardize the language  and releasing new version!
